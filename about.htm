Shark Tank

In 2022, our parent company, Akamai, asked us to develop a product to bring some of the features that Akamai offers to large corporate clients to Small Office Home Office (SOHO) networks. And LAN Sharks was born.

Local Area Networks, or LANs, that belong to small companies or even families can be threatened by malicious actors as much as big corporate networks. But where big companies have big budgets to mitigate vulnerabilies and detect attacks before they cause a lot of damage; the average SOHO owner doesn't have the same resources.

Until now.

Big companies have big budgets to mitigate vulnerabilies and detect attacks before they cause a lot of damage; the average SOHO owner doesn't have the same resources.

When you partner with LAN Sharks, you become a part of our family. We will bring our considerable skills in cybersecurity and put them to work on your behalf.

Lan Sharks software gives you unprecedented insight into your SOHO network. At a glance, our dashboard can tell you:

  • If any attacks have been detected on your network.
  • When the attack was detected.
  • The severity of the attack on a scale from 1-10.
  • A simple description of what happened.
  • How the threat could affect your network.

At LAN Sharks, we're really dedicated to the idea of empowering our end users. Yes, when you have problems with your network, we'll help you solve them. But if all we do is fix your problem, then we will feel our job isn't completely done.

Security isn't just about safety. It also involves feeling like you're in control, as well as actually being in control. How can you feel safe if we're the only ones that understand how your network works and how it's being secured?

Knowledge is power! And we are dedicated to help you increase your own knowledge of SOHO security. That's why for each alert on your dashboard, you will be able to jump to a description of the problem. If you call into our Help Desk (which is staffed 24/7), our representatives will explain what's going on. They'll give you that explaination in language you can understand without having to get a degreein cybersecurity. If you're interested, they will also provide you with references to resources you can use to increase your understanding. If you want to learn, we want to help!
